## 1. Product Vision and Mission

### Product Vision

Articulate the future state or ultimate aspiration of your product. It should be compelling and align with your company's broader vision.

- What does success look like in 1-3 years?
- How does this product transform the market or user experience?
- What is the long-term impact you want to achieve?

### Product Mission

Convey what your product aims to achieve for its users and how it contributes to realizing your Product Vision.

- What specific problem does your product solve?
- Who does it serve?
- How does it create value?
