## 1. Document Purpose

Explain what this guide is for and how it should be used.

This document translates the **strategic vision** of your product into **practical communication guidelines**.

It serves to:

- Guide how your brand communicates with users
- Ensure consistency across all channels (website, app, social media, ads, etc.)
- Avoid technical jargon or confusing language
- Enable consistent execution by anyone on the team (marketing, design, copy)

This guide is **operational**.  
It does not redefine your business model—it communicates it.

## 12. Consistency Checklist

Before publishing any text, ask:

- [ ] Does this sound [voice characteristic]?
- [ ] Would a [target audience type] understand this?
- [ ] Am I promising more than I can deliver?
- [ ] Is it clear that [key expectation limitation]?
- [ ] Does this align with our brand pillars?
- [ ] Is this consistent with our voice and tone guidelines?

If any answer is "no," revise.
