# Image Prompts for "Cursor para PMs" Presentation

**Style Guide for all prompts:**
- **Style:** Minimalist, modern corporate tech, "Notion-like" aesthetic.
- **Colors:** Dominant white/light grey, subtle textures, accent color #FF5757 (Product Arena Red).
- **Format:** 16:9 Aspect Ratio.
- **Constraint:** High negative space for text overlay.

## Slide 1: Title (Cursor para PMs)
**Prompt:** Minimalist abstract composition representing code meeting product management. Clean geometric shapes, white and light grey background with a single subtle red (#FF5757) dynamic line cutting through or highlighting a focal point. Modern tech aesthetic, high quality 3d render, soft lighting.

## Slide 2: Speaker Background
**Prompt:** Extremely subtle texture background, very light grey noise or soft gradient. Minimalist office glass texture, blurred completely. Serves as a clean backdrop for a profile slide.

## Slide 3: Filosofia (Copiloto)
**Prompt:** Abstract representation of a co-pilot concept. Two abstract paths or streams of light flowing in parallel and harmonizing. Minimalist, light background, red accent details on the "guide" path. ample negative space in the center.

## Slide 4: Como funciona
**Prompt:** Isometric 3D illustration of abstract digital building blocks being assembled. Minimalist style, white and light grey materials with small red indicators. Clean, high tech, engineering vibe but soft.

## Slide 5: Cursor vs Outros (Flow)
**Prompt:** Abstract network flow visualization. Smooth connecting lines between nodes, representing workflow automation. Light grey background, nodes are subtle circles. One path is highlighted in red.

## Slide 6: Pré-requisitos
**Prompt:** Minimalist illustration of a prepared digital workspace. Abstract icons of a laptop, a key (access), and a cloud (GitHub). Line art style, very subtle, light grey on white.

## Slide 7: Wow Moment (Jarvis)
**Prompt:** High-tech HUD interface ring, but adapted for a light-mode aesthetic. Clean, thin lines, futuristic but not dark/cyberpunk. White background with red interface elements.

## Slide 8: Setup / Workspace
**Prompt:** Abstract visualization of a file system or directory structure. Clean folders arranged logically. Minimalist 3D render, top-down view, soft shadows.

## Slide 16: Exercise - Drag and Drop
**Prompt:** Minimalist 3D render of a folder icon being dragged into a chat interface. Clean UI elements, "Notion-style" aesthetic. White background, soft shadows, cursor hand icon holding the folder. Red accent color #FF5757 on the folder or drop zone indicator.
